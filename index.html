Introducing the unofficial

Discogs Buttons

Display your Discogs profile on your website with these three simple social media buttons featuring dynamic collection, inventory, or want-list counts.

Collection

Let visitors know how many records are currently in your collection.

Wantlist

Display the current amount of records currently in your wantlist.

Inventory

Showcase the current number of records you have in your inventory.
